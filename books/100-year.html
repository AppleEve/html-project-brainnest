The 100-Year-Old Man Who Climbed Out the Window and Disappeared

This is one of the most hilarious and clever books I have ever red. Telling a colourful and eventful story of Allan Karlsson, it mixes his fantastic adventures with historical events in a Forest-Gump-like way. As funny as the movie may be, the book is so much more!
