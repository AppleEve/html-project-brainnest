Erast Fandorin Series

Detective novels by a Georgian writer, Boris Akunin, take you back to XIX-th century Russia. Full of mistery and unexpected plot-twists, it keeps you on the edge till the very end, Agatha Christie's style. Not to mention the very charismatic and wise main character, Erast Petrovich Fandorin himself!
