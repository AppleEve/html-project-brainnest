Master and Margerita

Master and Margerita were a school reading in high school. And as such, I expected a boring and out-dated novel that will keep me away from better things to do. Instead, I got an exquisite masterpiece full of black humour, philosophy and fantasy. I was so impressed, it became one of my favourite books and I return to it whenever I want my mind to be challanged. I find something new with every read!
