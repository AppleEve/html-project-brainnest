Como

The first time I've been to Como was as a student. I was visiting my friend in Milan and he brought me to Como as an attraction. I was absolutely stunned by the beauty of the Como lake and the charming city laying by it. Since my first visit I have been to Como 3 or 4 more times and it's always a must-go whenever in the Northern Italy.
