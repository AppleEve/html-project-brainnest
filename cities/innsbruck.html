Innsbruck

Austria will always and forever be the land of mountains and snow. And though of course it is not true, me and family would go every winter to ski in Austria, passing by the beautiful country covered in snow. On our way, we would pass a day or two in charming and heart-warming Innsbruck. Now that one of my best friends lives there, I still keep regularily visiting the city.
